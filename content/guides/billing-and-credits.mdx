## What Resets and What Doesn't

- **Monthly interviews, candidates, and jobs** reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused monthly allowance does not roll over.
- **Add-on interview credits** never expire. They carry over between billing periods and survive plan changes.
- **AI chat messages** reset daily.

## Plan Changes

### Upgrading
Upgrades take effect **immediately**. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing period, and your limits increase right away.

### Downgrading
Downgrades take effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You keep your current plan's limits until then.

### Cancelling
When you cancel, your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan. Your data is never deleted, but you won't be able to create new candidates or jobs beyond the free limits until you resubscribe.

### Add-on credits after plan changes
Add-on interview credits are not affected by plan changes. They remain in your account regardless of upgrades, downgrades, or cancellation.

## Payment & Invoicing

- Monthly subscriptions are charged **automatically** via Stripe

## Auto-Recharge

You can enable auto-recharge to automatically purchase add-on interview credits when your total available interviews drop below a threshold. Configure this in your billing settings:

- **Threshold**: When to trigger (5, 10, 25, or 50 credits remaining)
- **Pack**: Which add-on pack to purchase (Small, Medium, or Large)

Auto-recharge uses your saved payment method and charges automatically.
